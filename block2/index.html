Featured project: GVS - Global Virtual School

The Global Virtual School project is unique in that it connects, and in real-time, English-speaking volunteer teachers with students who are eager to improve their English to pursue post-secondary education, career training and better employment, all of which guarantee an escape from the cycle of generational poverty.

About WEF

WEF is a 501(c)3 registered in USA. The Dallas Foundation (TDF) is the fiscal sponsor of the Worldwide Education Fund of the Dallas Foundation (WEF), a component fund of The Dallas Foundation, a 501(c)(3), publicly supported charity, Federal Tax ID 75-2890371.

100% of all dollars raised through your contributions go towards implementation, operation, and maintenance of WEF’s projects and programs. All administrative expenses are paid by the Founders, Connie and Kamal Daya Gift Fund of The Dallas Foundation, and by our many generous, engaged volunteers.
